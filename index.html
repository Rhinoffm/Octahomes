About OctaHome — Dubai's Most Expensive Real Estate Tracker

OctaHome tracks every residential property transaction above AED 100 million in Dubai. In 2025, 47 of the most expensive homes in Dubai changed hands — including the record-breaking AED 550M Bugatti Residences penthouse in Business Bay, two AED 350M La Mer mega-villas in Jumeirah, and luxury waterfront estates on Palm Jumeirah, Jumeirah Bay, and the World Islands. Dubai's ultra-luxury real estate market has seen explosive growth, with nine-figure transactions increasing 2,160% from 2021 to 2025. The most expensive neighborhoods for luxury homes in Dubai include Jumeirah Second, Jumeirah First, Palm Jumeirah, DIFC, Business Bay, and Dubai Hills Estate, featuring branded residences from Bulgari, Aman, Four Seasons, and Bugatti.
